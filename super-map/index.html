Dar es Salaam Commercial Capital

Largest city and economic hub. Major port on Indian Ocean. Population: 6+ million. Known for vibrant nightlife, Kariakoo Market, Msasani Peninsula, and beautiful beaches.
Dodoma Capital City

Political capital since 1974. Houses Parliament and government ministries. Central location connecting all regions. Known for wine industry and parliament buildings.
Arusha Safari Gateway

Tourism capital at foot of Mount Meru. Gateway to Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, Tarangire. Diplomatic hub (East African Community HQ).
Mwanza Rock City

Second largest city on Lake Victoria shores. Major fishing port and industrial center. Known for massive granite boulders and vibrant waterfront.
Zanzibar Spice Islands

UNESCO Stone Town, white sand beaches, coral reefs. Historic trade center. Famous for cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg. Prison Island, Jozani Forest.
Mbeya Southern Highlands

Gateway to Malawi and Zambia. Coffee, tea, and banana production. Mbeya Peak and Poroto Mountains nearby. Cool mountain climate.
Moshi Kilimanjaro Base

Gateway to Africa's highest peak (5,895m). Coffee plantations, Chagga culture. Starting point for Machame, Marangu, Lemosho trekking routes.
Tanga Port City

Historic port city near Kenyan border. Pangani beaches, Amboni Caves. Less crowded coastal destination with Swahili ruins.
Kigoma Lake Tanganyika

Historic town on world's second deepest lake. Gombe Stream National Park (Jane Goodall chimpanzees). Burundi border nearby.
Morogoro Agricultural Hub

University town at foot of Uluguru Mountains. Sugarcane, rice production. Gateway to Mikumi National Park and Selous Game Reserve.
